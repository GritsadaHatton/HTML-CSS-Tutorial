Why You Should Donate Blood.

Keep being a lifesaver!

  1. You can save three people's lives with a single blood donation.
  2. Someone needs blood every three seconds.
  3. And one in three Aussies will require it at some point in their lives.
  4. That means one day it might be someone you love who needs it.
  5. Or, it might even be you.
  6. One in 10 people admitted to the hospital will need blood.
  7. One blood donation is needed every eight seconds to keep up with the demand from patients in Australia.
  8. Every drop of your blood contains roughly 200–300 million red blood cells.
  9. That means you have about 25 trillion red blood cells in your body (so you’re probably not going to miss a few).
  10. There are over 100 centres across Australia where you can donate.
